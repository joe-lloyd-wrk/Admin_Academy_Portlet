The Admin Academy

 

Patient Administration Admin Academy

 

Established in 2022 to develop training and standardisation of Elective Care patient administration processes.

Admin teams are pivotal to the management of patient pathways from referral, right through to discharge. The team are passionate about championing admin roles within our Trust and ensuring staff have access to substantial role-based training. Recognising the importance and impact training has on staff morale, performance, patient experience and patient safety.

Some of our work includes:

  • Back to basics - Identify areas requiring standardisation
  • Develop standard processes utilising SME knowledge from within the Trust
  • Develop and deliver training against agreed processes (Training delivered via classroom, online, and 1:1)
  • Ongoing check & verify, linking with our Trust Information Quality team to monitor data quality errors
  • Identify themes for continuous improvement.

    We have also developed the Admin Academy SharePoint to be a central hub for all training documents/resources. Our suite of Standard Operating Procedures (SOP's) and Work Instructions (underpinning each SOP) can be located via the SharePoint.

     

Standard Operating Procedure's

  • Consistency - If all staff members are following the same SOP's, to ensure we are all following the same standards and processes across the Trust
  • Efficiency - Easy to refer to saving staff members time trying to find answers when faced with difficulties on a particular they can easily refer to the procedures for direction
  • Safety - SOP's support standardisation with patient administration
  • Training and Onboarding - Robust SOP's compliment training for existing staff members and new starters.
  • Compliance - SOPs form a useful tool for supervisors/management to ensure teams are compliant and all following the same expected standard and required role-based responsibilities.
  • Audits and Regulation - Audit inspectors often check SOP's, their frequency of updates and degree of implementation. SOPs should easily acceptable and frequently reviewed and kept up to date.
  • Accountability - SOP's set who is responsible for the steps in a process. This ensures that everyone works to the best of their ability with accountability for their responsibilities.
  • Continuous Improvement - SOP's along with Information Quality measures can be used to measure and evaluate the performance of our Trust. This measurement allows for consistent review and improvement.
